Our Goals

The College of Information and Communications Technology seeks to develop globally competent ICT professionals, sufficiently equipped with appropriate knowledge, skills and attitude, for them to effectively design, develop, implement and manage information and communications technology resources in multi-disciplinary fields.

College Organizations

The different organizations serving the college.

  • Student Council College of Information and Communications Technology Student Council. Representative of the student body.
  • iCON The official student publication of the college.
  • Multimedia Majors Society Aims to provide its members additional knowledge, skills and attitude to creatively translate information resource into multimedia experience through the use of digital arts and multimedia packages and applications.
  • iCODE Organization specialized for learning how to implement, maintain and develop realiable and efficient software products or apps using software engineering concepts.
  • e-Pulse.net Test your skills in hardware and network troubleshooting. Help other people by providing technical support and connectivity.
  • Information Systems Organization Assist clients in developing Information Systems and provide assistance in organizing events.
  • LINK.exe Uphold, generate, enrich and serve - Link.exe is a special interest group whose members are trained through hands-on work and field projects within the university as well as external communities.
